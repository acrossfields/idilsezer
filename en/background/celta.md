  <p>By successfully completing the on-site full-time course at International House Bogotá, I was awarded the <em>Cambridge English Level 5 Certificate in Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages (CELTA)</em>&nbsp;with Grade B. In addition to extensive written assignments, this 4-week course involved 120 hours of classroom-based training comprising theoretical sessions, lesson observations, assisted lesson planning, teaching practice, and feedback sessions.</p>
